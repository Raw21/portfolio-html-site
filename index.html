Poppy Kidz
Poppy the Penguin loved to play, So much so, that he could play all day. With the other penguins his age, Poppy would play cops and robbers on ice… Throw a frisbee through the frigid air…
And determine who was the most athletic penguin of them all by running and jumping from ice sheet… To ice sheet… To ice sheet… To ice sheet… Until Poppy was the first one to make it to the end and declare his victory. “I WIN, I WIN…!” he shouted..
At the end of the day when his friends had no more play left in them and it was time to go home, one of the penguins said, “Well, see ya tomorrow, Poppy.” “See ya tomorrow,” Poppy said, frowning. He never wanted the day to end. But he always looked forward to playing again the next day..
